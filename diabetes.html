Developing the Balance Mat as a Screening Tool for Type 2 Diabetes

A research program investigating whether postural sway measurements captured by Balance Mat can identify patterns associated with Type 2 diabetes.

View Research Findings Partner With Us

2025/26 Research Program

A major research program conducted across two medical centres in NSW examined postural sway measurements from patients using Balance Mat technology.

🧪 210 patients assessed across two medical centres in NSW
📊 Postural sway metrics measured and analysed
🔍 15 patients demonstrated significantly different sway characteristics
🩺 The common marker among these 15 patients was Type 2 diabetes

Research Findings

The research identified a potential relationship between measured postural sway characteristics and Type 2 diabetes status. Further clinical validation is being undertaken.
