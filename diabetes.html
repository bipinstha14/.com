Exploring Balance Mat as a Screening Tool for Type 2 Diabetes

Balance and gait changes can be an early sign of diabetic peripheral neuropathy — nerve damage affecting the feet and legs that is common in people with type 2 diabetes. We're exploring how Balance Mat's objective, non-invasive sway data could support earlier screening.

Discuss This Application Partner With Us

Why Balance and Diabetes Are Connected

Diabetic peripheral neuropathy can gradually reduce sensation and stability in the feet and legs, which often shows up first as subtle changes in balance — sometimes before other symptoms are noticed.

✅ Non-invasive, quick screening (under 20 seconds)
✅ Objective postural sway data
✅ Potential for earlier identification of balance changes
✅ Repeatable testing to track changes over time
