Expanding the Possibilities Beyond Traditional Balance Assessment

Force plates have measured postural control for decades — but they demand complex calibration, dedicated lab space, and expensive infrastructure. Balance Mat reads balance through light instead of electricity, capturing the entire standing surface in a portable, point-of-care device.

Spec — Calibration

No Calibration Required

Strain-gauge systems drift with temperature and mechanical wear, requiring regular re-zeroing. Balance Mat reads micro-movements through changes in light transmission instead of electrical resistance — removing the drift problem entirely.
