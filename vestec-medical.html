Vestech Medical

A medical device innovation partner supporting the development and future commercialisation of Balance Mat.

About Vestech Medical

Vestech Medical is a medical device innovation partner that supports the translation of healthcare technologies from research into practical clinical solutions. With expertise spanning product development, regulatory strategy, commercialisation, and healthcare innovation, Vestech Medical contributes valuable knowledge to the advancement of emerging medical technologies.

As part of the Balance Mat partner network, Vestech Medical provides guidance on the pathway from research and clinical validation to real-world healthcare implementation. Their experience in medical device development complements Balance Mat's mission to deliver accessible, evidence-based balance assessment technology for clinicians, researchers, and healthcare providers.

Working Together

The collaboration between Balance Mat and Vestech Medical is focused on supporting the continued evolution of Balance Mat from an innovative research platform into a clinically deployable medical device. Together, we are exploring opportunities in product development, regulatory planning, commercialisation strategies, and expanding clinical adoption.

By combining Balance Mat's research expertise with Vestech Medical's experience in medical device innovation, the partnership aims to accelerate the delivery of reliable balance assessment solutions to healthcare settings.

Partnership Focus Area

  • Medical device development and innovation
  • Commercialisation planning
  • Regulatory strategy and quality systems
  • Clinical implementation support

Joint Initiatives

  • Supporting product refinement and development
  • Exploring clinical validation opportunities
  • Preparing for future regulatory pathways
  • Identifying commercial and healthcare partnerships

Shared Outcomes

  • Advancing evidence-based balance assessment technologies
  • Improving accessibility of clinical balance screening
  • Supporting healthcare professionals with practical assessment tools
  • Facilitating the translation of research into real-world clinical practice
